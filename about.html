The Minds Behind the Molds

We are a team of engineers, designers, and manufacturing experts dedicated to pushing the boundaries of plastic production.

Our Story: From Local Innovator to Industry Leader

Founded over a decade ago in Mayo, Florida, Mayo Plastics began with a simple mission: to provide higher quality, more reliable plastic components for local industries. We saw a need for a manufacturing partner that valued precision and partnership equally.

Through continuous investment in technology and talent, we have grown into a trusted supplier for businesses across the nation, known for our innovative solutions and unwavering commitment to client success.
