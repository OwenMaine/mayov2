Our Engineering Capabilities

Advanced processes and materials to bring your most demanding projects to life.

Injection Molding

We excel in creating highly complex and detailed parts through precision injection molding. Our state-of-the-art machinery handles a wide range of thermoplastic polymers, ensuring consistent quality and tight tolerances for high-volume production runs.

Custom Extrusion

Our custom extrusion services produce continuous shapes like tubing, piping, and seals. We work with you to develop custom dies, allowing for unique profiles tailored perfectly to your application, from simple designs to complex co-extrusions.

Recyclable Materials

Sustainability is at our core. We offer a range of recyclable and biodegradable material options, including PLA, PETG, and post-consumer resins. Our team can help you select the right eco-friendly material without compromising performance.

OEM Fulfillment

We operate as a seamless extension of your team. From production to packaging and logistics, our OEM fulfillment services ensure your components are manufactured, assembled, and delivered on-spec and on-time, directly to your production line or end-users.
